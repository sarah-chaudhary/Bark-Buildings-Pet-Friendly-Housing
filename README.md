Bark Buildings is a WordPress website showcasing residential housing solutions designed for pet‑friendly communities.

The platform was built to highlight property features, community benefits, and services while enabling inquiries from residents and property managers.

Key development work included:
• Custom WordPress layout for property and service presentation
• Structured service and community pages
• Inquiry forms for residents and property managers
• Mobile‑responsive navigation for property browsing

The website effectively communicates the concept of pet‑friendly housing while supporting lead generation for property management teams.
Bark Buildings showcases pet‑friendly residential housing solutions designed for communities that support pet owners. The platform highlights services, community features, and inquiry systems that help residents and property managers manage pet‑friendly environments.
Bark Buildings provides pet-friendly residential housing solutions with services designed for both residents and property managers.
